Our Lawyers

Tim J. Harmar, BA (HONS), JD, MBA, LLM, LLM, M.Eng., CAPP

Founder & Principal Lawyer – Corporate, Intellectual Property & Technology Law

Tim J. Harmar is an award-winning lawyer and the driving force behind NorthernEdge Legal Solutions. With a distinguished record in corporate, intellectual property, and technology law—including landmark cases before the Supreme Court of Canada—Tim also educates future legal professionals as a professor at Sault College of Applied Arts and Technology.

Throughout his career, Tim has built a reputation for innovative problem-solving, persuasive advocacy, and a commitment to excellence. His experience ranges from high-profile civil litigation, including appearances before the Supreme Court of Canada, to advising on complex corporate transactions and intellectual property matters. Tim has also contributed to academic institutions as a professor, enriching the field of law through teaching and community engagement.

At NorthernEdge Legal Solutions, Tim provides all legal services personally, ensuring that every client benefits from his direct involvement and a highly personalized approach. His practice is founded on the principles of integrity, diligence, and a relentless pursuit of favorable outcomes for every client, no matter the complexity of their case.
