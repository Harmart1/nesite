Professional Biography

I am Tim J. Harmar, Founder and Principal Lawyer at NorthernEdge Legal Solutions. With an extensive academic and professional background—including a BA (Hons), JD, MBA, two LLM degrees, and a Master of Engineering—I bring a unique combination of legal expertise and technical knowledge to serve clients throughout Northern Ontario.

Throughout my career, I have built a reputation for innovative problem-solving, persuasive advocacy, and a commitment to excellence. My experience ranges from high-profile litigation cases to complex business transactions and cutting-edge technology law matters. This diverse background allows me to approach legal challenges with a multidisciplinary perspective.

At NorthernEdge Legal Solutions, I provide all legal services personally, ensuring that every client benefits from my direct involvement and a highly personalized approach. I believe in building long-term relationships with my clients based on trust, reliability, and delivering consistent results.
