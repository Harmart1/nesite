Professional Biography

I am Tim J. Harmar, Founder and Principal Lawyer at NorthernEdge Legal Solutions. With an extensive academic and professional background—including a BA (Hons), JD, MBA, two LLMs, and an M.Eng.—I have dedicated my career to providing award-winning legal services that span corporate and intellectual property law, as well as emerging areas of technology and cybersecurity.

Throughout my career, I have built a reputation for innovative problem-solving, persuasive advocacy, and a commitment to excellence. My experience ranges from high-profile civil litigation, including appearances before the Supreme Court of Canada, to advising on complex corporate transactions and intellectual property matters. I have also contributed to academic institutions as a professor, enriching the field of law through teaching and community engagement.

At NorthernEdge Legal Solutions, I provide all legal services personally, ensuring that every client benefits from my direct involvement and a highly personalized approach. My practice is founded on the principles of integrity, diligence, and a relentless pursuit of favorable outcomes for every client, no matter the complexity of their case.
